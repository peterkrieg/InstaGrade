Privacy

We Take Your Privacy Very Seriously

  1. 1.None of your Instagram media is stored. MediaScore only stores URL's of your photos and videos. Because these are connected directly to Instagram, any changes you make through Instagram will instantly be reflected in MediaScore.
  2. 2.Only you can view your Instagram media and information. This is not viewable to any other users.
  3. 3.We don't store any Instagram login credentials or sensitive information. We store minimal info about liked media, follows/followers, tagged locations, and generated scores of reports. This is necessary to record snapshots of your account over time, and to have much faster subsequent logins.
  4. 4.We don't store any of your information without your explicit permission. If you for any reason decide to delete your account, all of your information is permanently removed from our servers.
  5. 5.We don't share any information with external sources. The small amount of information MediaScore saves is never shared with other companies.
